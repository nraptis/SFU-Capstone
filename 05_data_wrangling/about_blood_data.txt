Notes about this data.

I simply searched around on the internet and grabbed every blood cell dataset I could get my hands on.

The main things I was looking for:

1.) Primarily single cell images.
2.) Large enough to shrink down.
3.) Sharp enough to not be super blurry.

----

Is this data "ready to go?"

It's your call. For my own project, I intend to sift through this data.
For example, in the "120 patient" data, there are a lot of images with more than one cell.

Some of the non-smudge-cell images could be considered smudge cells - depending on the doctor.

No credit is given to the sources, I have converted and/or renamed the files.

Please track down and give credit to the original data sets if you're using these images.

I am not aware of any legal ties, these are derived from publically available data.
